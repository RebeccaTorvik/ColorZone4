About

Welcome to The Color Zone Salon! We have been in business 11 years, located in the Kentlands community in Gaithersburg, Maryland. Our salon mission is to create a look you can maintain at home, so you can feel your best. We understand that when your hair looks good, you feel good. Continuing education is a large part of our salon's success. Our industry is changing all of the time and the only way to grow is to keep learning.
